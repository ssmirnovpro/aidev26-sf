## Products

### Agentic Document Extraction Platform

An API-first platform for extracting data from complex documents:
- Extracts data from form fields, tables, and charts
- Multimodal document processing
- Transforms "dark data" into structured, auditable information
- Supports complex layouts

### Computer Vision Platform

A platform for visual AI:
- Build, train, and deploy computer vision models
- Analyze images and video
- Minimal data requirements
- Streamlined MLOps

### Industry Applications

- Finance and insurance
- Healthcare
- Energy
- Legal
- Logistics

## Business Model

**Model: API Platform (SaaS) + Enterprise Contracts**

- **API-first approach** — credit-based pricing for document processing tasks (pay-as-you-go or monthly credit plans)
- **Enterprise contracts** — custom integrations for large clients
- **Self-serve API** — for developers and smaller teams

**Revenue sources:**
- API consumption (credits consumed by document-processing tasks/pages)
- Enterprise licenses
- Custom solutions for regulated industries

## Analysis

LandingAI is part of Andrew Ng's AI Fund portfolio. The company has evolved its focus from general computer vision to document AI—positioning itself at the intersection of document processing and agentic workflows. The shift aligns with growing enterprise demand for agents that can extract and process information from complex documents.
