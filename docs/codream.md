# Codream

## About the Company

**Codream** (codream.ai) is a new platform for collaborative learning and building AI applications. Billing itself as "A New Learning Experience," Codream launched in preview mode in April 2026.

Andrew Ng is the founder of DeepLearning.AI, co-founder of Coursera, former head of Google Brain, former Chief Scientist at Baidu, and Managing Partner at AI Fund. He's one of the most influential figures in AI education and entrepreneurship.

## AI Dev 26 Launch

**Andrew Ng himself unveiled Codream on stage at AI Dev 26** in San Francisco (April 28–29, 2026), a conference for AI developers organized by DeepLearning.AI that drew over 3,000 attendees. Codream's launch was one of the key announcements during his keynote.

## Product

**Codream** is a platform for collaborative learning and development with AI. It launched in preview at the AI Dev 26 conference.

### Key Features

Based on the conference presentation:

- Collaborative work with AI to build applications
- Targeted at developers and people learning to work with AI
- Integrated with the DeepLearning.AI ecosystem
- Available at codream.ai

## Funding

Codream hasn't disclosed funding details. The product is linked to Andrew Ng's broader ecosystem:

- **AI Fund** — Andrew Ng's venture studio, managing over $370 million. In 2025, the firm closed a new $190 million fund (oversubscribed). AI Fund co-founds AI companies from scratch. Backers include Sequoia Capital, NEA, SoftBank, Nikkei, and AES.
- **DeepLearning.AI** — an educational platform with over 7 million learners, 700+ events, and 70K+ participants.

Codream appears to be a project from the DeepLearning.AI / AI Fund ecosystem.

## Business Model

Codream launched in preview in April 2026. The company hasn't announced a specific monetization strategy yet. Looking at other DeepLearning.AI products:

- **DeepLearning.AI Pro** — a membership subscription for AI builders
- Free short courses plus paid specializations through Coursera
- A freemium model seems likely: free basic access with premium features available via subscription
