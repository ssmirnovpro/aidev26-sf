# Actian

## About the Company

**Actian** is the data and AI division of HCLSoftware. They're focused on data management, analytics, and database solutions. With more than 30 years in the business, Actian positions itself as a data layer engineering platform for enterprise AI.

## Participation in AI Dev 26

**Speaker:** Emma McGrattan — CTO

**Talk Topic:** "Engineering the Context Layer: Vector Databases Across Cloud, Edge, and On-Prem AI"

**Focus:** Data sovereignty, regulated industries, and the importance of hybrid infrastructure (cloud, edge, on-premises) for enterprise AI.

### About the Speaker

Emma McGrattan was promoted to CTO in February 2025 after spending 30 years at Actian, starting out as an associate engineer. She's the author of "Vector Databases for Enterprise AI" (O'Reilly).

## Products

### VectorAI DB

A new enterprise vector database (first unveiled in January 2026, officially launched at AI Dev 26 on April 28, 2026):

- **More than 22x faster** vector search in Actian's benchmarks (VectorDBBench tests against Milvus and Qdrant Local on identical self-hosted hardware)
- Optimized for **on-premises, edge, and self-hosted** environments
- Built for regulated industries: healthcare, government
- Greater control over data and AI compared to cloud solutions

### Actian Data Platform

A comprehensive data management platform:
- Analytics
- Data integration
- Data management
- Support for hybrid and multi-cloud environments

### Key Databases

- **Actian Ingres** — relational database
- **Actian Analytics Engine** (formerly Actian Vector) — columnar analytics database
- **Actian Zen** — embedded database
- **VectorAI DB** — new AI-specialized database

## Analysis

Actian is positioning VectorAI DB around local-first, edge, on-premises, and disconnected deployments, contrasting it with cloud-first vector database offerings. Their focus on data sovereignty and regulated industries (healthcare, financial services, government, manufacturing) gives them a strategic edge in regions with strict data regulations. Being part of HCLSoftware means they've got access to established enterprise sales channels and global IT infrastructure.
